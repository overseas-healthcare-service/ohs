Overseas Healthcare Service

The Overseas Healthcare Service enables insured citizens to get state funded healthcare when abroad.

About this service

Use this service to certify you are eligible for UK state funded healthcare abroad, or to register as a foreign national for healthcare in the UK. If you paid for healthcare when abroad use this service to claim back costs.

Get state healthcare abroad

Eligible people from the UK can get state funded healthcare when abroad. Use this service to certify you are eligible for state funded healthcare abroad.

Register for state healthcare in the UK

Insured and eligible people from the EU/EEA and Switzerland can use this service to register for state healthcare while in the UK.

  • Register for state healthcare in the UK

Tell us your circumstances have changed

If your circumstances have changed use this service to let us know. Tell us you have changed address, moved abroad, returned to the UK, or you no longer need overseas healthcare.

  • Tell us your circumstances have changed

Claim for costs of healthcare abroad

You may be eligible to claim for healthcare costs while abroad in participating countries.
